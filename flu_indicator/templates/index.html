Weekly Influenza Predictor





In this section we explored with different models of Machine Learning to try and predict weekly how many patients around the nation will need aid for Influenza-Like Illness. There are many models we tried, all with varied results. Gradient Boosting Regression became our most successful model so we dove a little farther into the data to see how our predictions did!
